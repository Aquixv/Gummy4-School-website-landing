Asset & Copyright Guide
To ensure this template is lightweight and legally compliant, specific branded assets have been removed. Please follow these steps to personalize your portal:

Images & Media: All original photos and videos containing real people or specific university branding have been deleted to avoid copyright issues.

Action Required: You must replace the placeholder paths in your React components (e.g., src/assets/images/) with your own files.

Icons: FontAwesome/Bootstrap icons remain integrated as they are open-source and safe for template redistribution.

Forms: The contact section is set up with W3Forms. Simply replace "YOUR_KEY" in your form component with your unique API key from W3Forms.
